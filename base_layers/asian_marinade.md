Asian Style Tofu or Pork Marinade
=================================

For marinade mix:

* quarter cup lime juice
* quarter cup soy sauce
* quarter cup toasted sesame oil
* two cloves of garlic, chopped
* 3 teaspoons cumin
* 1 teaspoon paprika
* Some chopped cilantro
