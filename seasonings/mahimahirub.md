Mahi Mahi Rub
==============

A rub for Mahi Mahi, if fish tacos be what you're fancying:

* 1 Part spicy chili powder
* 1 Part salt
* 1 Part ground cumin
* 1/2 Part chopped cilantro
* 1/2 Part cayenne
* 1/2 Part pepper
* 1/2 Part oregano
* 1/2 Part onion powder
* 1/2 Part garlic powder

You choose your measurements and enjoy!
